Refund Policies

Last Updated: 20th April 2026

This document outlines our terms for service cancellations and refunds for clients at Innoveloper, accessible from innoveloper.com.

1. Refund and Cancellation Policy

This policy outlines how you can cancel or seek a refund for a product or service purchased through our Platform:

  • Cancellation Requests: Cancellations will only be considered if the request is made within 1 day of placing the order. However, requests will not be entertained once the order has been communicated to the service provider/merchant and they have initiated the process of shipping or service provision.
  • Perishable Items: We do not accept cancellation requests for perishable items like flowers or eatables. A refund or replacement may only be issued if the user establishes that the quality of the delivered product was not satisfactory.
  • Damaged or Defective Items: Please report any damaged or defective items to our customer service team within 1 day of receipt. Requests will be processed once the merchant has inspected and determined the condition at their end.
  • Expectation Discrepancies: If you feel a product received is not as shown on the site or does not meet your expectations, you must notify customer service within 1 day of receiving the product.
  • Refund Processing: For any approved refunds, it will take 1 day for the refund to be processed back to you.

2. Return Policy

Our return and exchange parameters are as follows:

  • Eligibility Window: We offer refunds or exchanges within the first 1 day from the date of your purchase. After 1 day, no return, exchange, or refund will be offered.
  • Condition: To be eligible, the item must be unused, in the same condition as received, and in its original packaging. Items purchased on sale may not be eligible for return.
  • Exemptions: Certain categories of products are exempted from returns or refunds and will be identified at the time of purchase.

3. Contact Us

If you have any questions about these Refund Terms, please contact us at: contact@innoveloper.com
