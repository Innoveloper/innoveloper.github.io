Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 10th March, 2026

At Innoveloper, accessible from innoveloper.com, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors and clients. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by Innoveloper and how we use it in the context of our software development, web design, and graphic services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to all our clients. The types of information we collect include:

Personal & Business Information:

  • Contact details such as name, email address, phone number, and company name when you inquire about our services.
  • Project-related data, including design assets, source code (if provided), business requirements, and branding guidelines.
  • Payment information and billing addresses for processing service invoices.

Technical & Usage Information:

  • Log files and cookies to analyze website traffic and user behavior on our site.
  • Device information (browser type, IP address, time zone) to optimize our web experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used in the following ways:

  • To provide, operate, and maintain our development and design services.
  • To improve and personalize your experience on our website.
  • To communicate with you regarding project updates, support, and technical queries.
  • To process transactions and send invoices.
  • To send periodic newsletters or promotional materials (only with your consent).
  • To prevent fraudulent activities and ensure the security of our digital assets.

3. Confidentiality and Data Protection

As an IT service provider, we understand the sensitivity of your project data:

  • NDAs: We are committed to signing Non-Disclosure Agreements (NDAs) to protect your intellectual property.
  • Storage: Client project files and source code are stored on secure, encrypted servers with restricted access.
  • Third Parties: We do not sell or trade your personal information. We only share data with trusted third-party tools (like hosting providers or payment processors) necessary to fulfill your project requirements.

4. Data Retention

We retain client information for as long as necessary to provide the services requested, or for legal, tax, and accounting purposes. Upon project completion and request, we can perform a data purge of project-specific assets, subject to any legal obligations to maintain records.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of any inaccurate information.
  • Request the deletion of your data from our active marketing databases.
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: contact@innoveloper.com
